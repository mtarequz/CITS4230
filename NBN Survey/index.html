Welcome to the NBN Survey

 

This site has been developed for the UWA School of Computer Science unit CITS4230, it's purpose is to gather useful data on the proposed Australian National Broadband Network (NBN) by way of a simple survey. The data we gather will then be displayed in a useful manner which will serve to highlight the public's opinions of the proposed network.

The NBN is an Australian Government initiative and represents the largest ever infrastructure project undertaken in this country. It is an Australian Government initiative which will deliver high speed broadband to all Australians. The NBN is a new, wholesale only, open access, high speed broadband network.

The proposed infrastructure will require the laying of fibre optic cabling to at least 90 per cent of Australian homes, schools and businesses, capable of delivering speeds of 100 megabits per second, or up to 100 times faster than many people experience today. The remaining premises will be connected via a combination of next generation high speed wireless and satellite technologies delivering broadband speeds of 12 megabits per second or more.
